About

Dr. Kakirala Bindu Pavani

Dr. Bindu Pavani is a highly accomplished Consultant Obstetrician and Gynaecologist with over eight years of dedicated practice in women's healthcare. She brings together the precision of modern evidence-based medicine and the warmth of patient-centred care.

She currently consults at Padma Nursing Home (Karmanghat) and Vaidyalaya Speciality Clinics, Hyderabad.

Qualifications
MBBS — RIMS Adilabad | DGO — Gandhi Medical College, Secunderabad | DNB (OBG) — Fernandez Foundation, Hyderabad (2022). TSMC Reg. No. 93853.
Fertility & IVF
Comprehensive fertility investigations, ovulation induction, IUI, and complete IVF support with individualised protocols.
Surgical Expertise
Proficient in minimally invasive laparoscopic procedures for ovarian cysts, fibroids, endometriosis, and ectopic pregnancies.
